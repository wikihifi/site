About wikihifi.com What is wikihifi.com? Wikipedia is a fairly well organized knowledge repository. Unstructured text search engines are not the best way to gain this knowledge. Also, Google's and 'Wikipedia's own search are geared towards finding content that the you already know about. wikihifi.com helps you discover Wikipedia's content. It lets you research a topic by tuning categories, content, title, references etc - the way you tune your hifi system (or radios in good olden days). Current wikipedia search engines do not visualize results well. Wikipedia's own search is article title driven. Google's search results are not tailored to Wikipedia's content but general web content. They expect the users to refine their own search without providing any help. wikihifi.com assists the user in their research.
